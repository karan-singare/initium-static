About Us

INITIUM is the National Mining Technological Festival of the Department of Mining Engineering, IIEST, Shibpur. Started back in 2012, INITIUM 2020 will be the 6th edition of this successful extravaganza to be held on February 5-6, 2020. We expect wide scale participation from the industry and academia. There are events like Enviro Case Study, Mino Case Study and Paper Presentation by students and much more. Seminar speakers will be from eminent industries.It is based on the theme of Mining and Allied Fields.

  • Modern technologies technologies in underground underground and opencast coal mining
  • Advances in exploration techniques
  • Modern IT enabled systems in mining industry.
  • Advances in coal and mineral benificiation.
  • Challenges and Advances to exploitation of ferrous and non=ferrous minerals.

Enviro Case Study

  • Only teams whose members have registered online would be able to participate.
  • Each team can contain a maximum of four (4) participants. Students from different departments and colleges can form a team.Only teams whose members have registered online would be able to participate.
  • The abstract submission has to be a word document which should be mailed to initium.mining@gmail.com with subject titled enviro.
  • Teams should mention their participants name and their college name on the first page of the abstract.
  • For the preliminary round each team has to prepare an abstract of the solution based on the problem statement.
  • The abstract should not be longer than 2 pages.
  • The whole analysis of the problem statement should be done by the participants. Plagiarism will attract cancellation of participation.
  • Teams presenting their analysis in the second round should come with proper presentation along with their word document submitted in first round.
  • Each team will be given a time of 10 minutes to present.
  • Teams qualified for second round are required to bring 3 printed copies of their presentation and needed to be submitted before the event starts.
  • The decision of the judges would be final and binding
  • Team INITIUM6.0 reserves the right to change the rules and regulations of the event. You are accordingly advised to keep visiting the website
  • Only top three (3) teams will get certificate of merit. However other teams would get a certificate of participation.
